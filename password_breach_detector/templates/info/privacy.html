Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critical to us. We build security tools on the absolute principle that your plain text credentials must never be exposed.

Data Transmission & Hash Checks

When using our k-Anonymity checks, only the first 5 characters of your SHA-1 password hash are sent to the external HaveIBeenPwned database. The rest is checked inside your local browser. The raw text password is never stored or logged in our databases.
